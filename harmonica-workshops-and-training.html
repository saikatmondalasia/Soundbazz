About Our Workshops

Sound Bazz conducts immersive harmonica workshops across India, bringing the joy of music to enthusiasts of all levels. Our workshops are designed to be hands-on, engaging, and deeply inspiring — whether you're a complete beginner picking up the harmonica for the very first time or a seasoned player looking to refine your craft.

From breathing techniques and note formation to playing songs and stage performance — each workshop is a complete musical experience. Participants get to learn directly from experienced instructors, enjoy live demonstrations, interact with fellow harmonica lovers, and take home lasting skills and memories. Over the years, Sound Bazz has proudly hosted workshops in multiple cities across India, creating a growing community of harmonica enthusiasts.
